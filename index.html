👤 关于我About Me

我是厦门大学Mac实验室博士研究生（计算机科学与技术），研究方向为人工智能与计算机视觉。 在导师孙晓帅教授、纪荣嵘教授的指导下，我的研究兴趣包含底层视觉、目标分割/检测、多模态大模型表征学习等。 迄今为止，我已在CVPR、AAAI、PR、TGARS、ICANN等国际会议和期刊发表9篇论文，并拥有3项国家发明专利。 我热爱技术研究，致力于将理论成果转化为实际应用，解决真实世界中的复杂问题。

深度学习 计算机视觉 多模态大模型 图像分割/检测

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Xiamen University, specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision. Under the supervision of Prof. Xiaoshuai Sun and Prof. Rongrong Ji, my research focuses on low-level vision, object segmentation/detection, and multi-modal large model representation learning. To date, I have published 9 papers in top-tier conferences and journals such as CVPR, AAAI, PR, TGARS, and ICANN, and hold 4 national invention patents. I am passionate about research and dedicated to translating theoretical advancements into practical applications to solve complex real-world problems.

Deep Learning Computer Vision Multi-modal Large Models Image Segmentation/Detection
